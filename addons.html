Golden ratio calculator

I punched together a quick calculator that should give you a ballpark range of how many calories you should be taking in to reach your goal as efficiently as possible. 

What exactly do I mean by efficiently as possible? Well, I started out by using the Katch McArdle formula which is well recognized as a great starting point, and then skewed the results slightly to help you cut faster, and bulk slower.
